Osage RedSuns

A Software Engineering Powerhouse

The Osage RedSuns (オーセージ レッドサンズ) is a highly skilled group of software engineers. The group was created with the purpose of dominating the hackathon scene all over the United States, recruiting the residents of a hut on Osage Street and their friends to achieve this goal. While it is vocalized that the purpose of the Osage RedSuns is to establish a legend, it is apparent that the primary reason is to develop the skills of its members to a new and extraordinary level.
